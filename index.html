Marrying the Client & the Clinic

    
The What & The Way — Staff Are the Hinge

    

      Clients and the clinic want the same two things. The What — the medicine: diagnosis, recommendations, cost, follow-through. The Way — how it's delivered: kindness, listening, clear framing, written plans.
